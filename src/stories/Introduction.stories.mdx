# SERASI DESIGN SYSTEM

<Subtitle>SERASI Design System was created to help us build products for our customers more efficiently.</Subtitle>

<Subtitle>Why we need a Design System?</Subtitle>

### **Consistency**

Consistency is one of the core principles of proper design and user experience.
A consistent design is intuitive, predictable and easy to understand.

Benefits:

- Became a single source of truth for product and engineering teams
- Improves code and design quality that are well tested, flexible and ready for use
- Improves user experience as a result of consistent components, design interactions, and patterns

### **Efficiency**

To increase the speed at which product teams produce new or improved components, and reduce duplication.

Benefits:

- Better knowledge sharing
- Improves velocity for designers and developers working across teams
- Reduces duplication of work
- Reduces development time, so we cant focust on bussiness logic and product development

### **Collaboration**

Helps developers, designers, UX engineers and clients to share a single shared source of truth.

Benefits:

- Gets everyone on the same page
- Improves collaboration through common tools and language
- Makes the on-boarding process faster for new team members
